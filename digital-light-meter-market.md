<p><strong>The Digital Light Meter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Reflected-Light</li><li>Incident-Light</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Digital Light Meter market can be segmented into two main types: reflected-light and incident-light meters. Reflected-light meters measure the light that bounces off a surface, making them ideal for photography and surface evaluation. In contrast, incident-light meters gauge the light falling directly onto a subject, providing accurate readings for exposure settings in photography and videography. Both types are essential tools across various industries, including photography, cinematography, and architectural lighting design, catering to different lighting measurement needs.</p></p>
