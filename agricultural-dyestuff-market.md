<p><p>The agricultural dyestuff market can be categorized into two primary types: solution and powder. Solution dyestuffs are liquid formulations that dissolve easily in water, making them suitable for direct application in various agricultural processes, such as seed treatment or crop protection. Conversely, powder dyestuffs are dry formulations that require mixing with solvents or water before use. They offer versatility in application methods and are often favored for bulk use. Both types serve crucial roles in enhancing crop quality and productivity.</p></p>
