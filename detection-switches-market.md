<p><p>Detection switches are crucial components in various applications, categorized primarily into Single Pole Double Throw (SPDT) and Single Pole Single Throw Normally Closed (SPST-NC) types. SPDT switches allow for the connection of a single input to one of two outputs, enabling versatile circuit configurations. In contrast, SPST-NC switches remain closed until activated, making them ideal for safety and alarm systems. These market types cater to industries like automation, security, and consumer electronics, enhancing operational efficiency and reliability.</p></p>
