<p><p>The Solar Polysilicon Ingot Wafer Cell Module market features two primary types of configurations: series connection and parallel connection. In a series connection, individual solar cells are linked one after another, increasing voltage while maintaining current, ideal for high voltage applications. Conversely, the parallel connection links cells side by side, enhancing current while keeping voltage constant, suitable for lower voltage systems. These configurations influence overall performance, efficiency, and suitability for specific solar energy applications and installations.</p></p>
