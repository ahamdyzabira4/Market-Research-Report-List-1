<p><strong>The Hydro Turbine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Impact Turbine</li><li>Counterattack Turbine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hydro turbine market primarily includes impact turbines and counter-attack turbines. Impact turbines, like Pelton wheels, utilize high-velocity water jets, converting kinetic energy into mechanical energy, making them ideal for high-head, low-flow sites. Counter-attack turbines, such as Francis turbines, combine both kinetic and potential energy, functioning efficiently across varying flow and head conditions. Both types cater to diverse hydropower applications, contributing significantly to renewable energy generation and environmental sustainability while serving distinct operational requirements in hydroelectric plants.</p></p>
