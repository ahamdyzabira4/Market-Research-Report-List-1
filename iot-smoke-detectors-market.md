<p><strong>The IOT Smoke Detectors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Photoelectric</li><li>Ionization</li><li>Combination</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The IoT smoke detector market comprises three main types: photoelectric, ionization, and combination detectors. Photoelectric detectors use light beams to identify smoldering fires, making them effective for detecting slow-burning fires. Ionization detectors contain radioactive material that senses fast-flaming fires by detecting changes in electrical currents. Combination detectors integrate both technologies, offering enhanced sensitivity to various fire types, providing comprehensive safety solutions. The rise of IoT capabilities enhances their functionality, enabling remote monitoring and alerts for improved safety management.</p></p>
