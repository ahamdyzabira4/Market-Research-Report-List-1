<p><strong>The Optical Ground Wire (OPGW) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Central Tube Structure OPGW</li><li>Layer Stranding Structure OPGW</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Optical Ground Wire (OPGW) is a cable that combines traditional grounding capabilities with optical fiber communication. The market features two main types: Central Tube Structure OPGW and Layer Stranding Structure OPGW. Central Tube Structure OPGW has optical fibers housed within a central tube, offering better protection and performance against environmental factors. In contrast, Layer Stranding Structure OPGW consists of fiber strands embedded in layers around a metallic core, providing flexibility and ease of installation, particularly in existing infrastructure.</p></p>
