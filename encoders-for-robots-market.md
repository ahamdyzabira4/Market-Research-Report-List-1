<p><strong>The Encoders for Robots Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Absolute Encoders</li><li>Relative Encoders</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The robot encoders market is primarily segmented into two types: absolute and relative encoders. Absolute encoders provide a unique position value for each angle of rotation, ensuring precise positioning even after power loss, making them ideal for applications requiring high accuracy. In contrast, relative encoders, also known as incremental encoders, measure changes in position and require a reference point to determine the absolute position. Each type caters to different operational needs, influencing their adoption in robotics and automation.</p></p>
