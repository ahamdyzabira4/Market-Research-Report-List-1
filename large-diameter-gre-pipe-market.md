<p><p>The Large-Diameter GRE (Glass Reinforced Epoxy) Pipe market is segmented into two primary types: Hot Rolled Steel Pipe and Cold Drawn Pipe. Hot Rolled Steel Pipes are produced by rolling steel at high temperatures, offering better ductility and reduced production costs, making them suitable for large-scale applications. In contrast, Cold Drawn Pipes are manufactured at room temperature, providing improved dimensional accuracy, surface finish, and strength, often used in applications requiring tighter tolerances and enhanced mechanical properties.</p></p>
