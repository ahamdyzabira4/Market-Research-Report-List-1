<p><p>The synthetic gear oils market includes **Fully Synthetic Oil** and **Semi-Synthetic Oil**. Fully synthetic oils are engineered from chemically modified petroleum components or entirely synthetic compounds, offering superior performance, thermal stability, and extended service life, ideal for high-stress applications. Semi-synthetic oils blend synthetic base oils with conventional mineral oils, providing a balance of performance and cost-effectiveness, suitable for moderate operating conditions. Both types enhance lubrication, reduce wear, and improve efficiency, catering to diverse industrial and automotive gear systems.</p></p>
