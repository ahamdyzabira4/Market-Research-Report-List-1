<p><p>Advanced Metering Architecture (AMA) encompasses a framework for efficient energy and utility management through smart metering technologies. The Smart Meters are devices that measure and communicate energy usage in real-time. The Wide Area Communication Network facilitates data transmission between smart meters and utility providers over large distances. The Measurement Data Management System processes and analyzes this data for billing and optimization. The User Indoor Network allows end-users to monitor their energy consumption via connected devices, enhancing user engagement and energy efficiency.</p></p>
