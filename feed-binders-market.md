<p><p>Feed binders are substances used in animal feed to improve pellet quality, enhance nutrient retention, and reduce feed wastage. They act as binding agents, ensuring the feed maintains its structural integrity during handling, storage, and consumption. Common feed binders include natural binders like lignin, hemicellulose, and gelatin, as well as synthetic options such as bentonite and guar gum. These binders are widely used in livestock, poultry, aquaculture, and pet food industries to optimize feed efficiency and animal health.</p><p>The Feed Binders Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.9% during the forecast period. This growth is driven by increasing demand for high-quality animal feed, rising awareness about animal nutrition, and the expansion of the livestock and aquaculture industries.
