<p><p>The tellurium sputtering target market comprises two main types: rotary and non-rotatable targets. Rotary targets are designed to rotate during the sputtering process, providing even material distribution and longer operational life, making them suitable for high-volume production. Non-rotatable targets, on the other hand, are stationary and typically used for smaller-scale applications or specialized processes. Both types cater to different industry needs, including semiconductor manufacturing and thin-film technologies, ensuring flexibility in material deposition methods.</p></p>
