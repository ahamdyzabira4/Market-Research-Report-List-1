<p><p>The pedometer market comprises two primary types: smart pedometers and manual pedometers. Smart pedometers, often integrated with smartphones or fitness trackers, utilize advanced technology like GPS and Bluetooth to track steps, distance, and even heart rate, providing real-time data and insights for users. In contrast, manual pedometers are simpler, mechanical devices that count steps through a basic mechanical mechanism or accelerometer without the need for connectivity, making them more affordable and user-friendly for individuals seeking straightforward step tracking.</p></p>
